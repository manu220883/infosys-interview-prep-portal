Executive-Level Interview Questions & Answers

1. Explain the lifecycle of a service desk ticket.

A service desk ticket lifecycle starts from ticket creation through multiple stages including categorization, prioritization, assignment, investigation, resolution, validation, and closure. Effective governance ensures SLA adherence, accurate documentation, escalation management, and customer communication throughout the lifecycle.
Keywords: SLA MTTR FCR Escalation Matrix

2. How do you manage a critical P1 incident?

I initiate the Major Incident Management process immediately by establishing a war room, assigning technical owners, engaging stakeholders, and driving regular executive communications. My focus remains on service restoration, risk mitigation, stakeholder confidence, and conducting post-incident RCA with preventive actions.
Mention business continuity, executive updates, customer impact mitigation, and post-incident governance.

3. How do you improve customer satisfaction in support operations?

I focus on proactive communication, faster resolution times, knowledge management, agent enablement, and data-driven operational improvements. I regularly analyze CSAT feedback, identify recurring pain points, and implement targeted service improvement initiatives.
Discuss CSAT trends, proactive engagement, service reviews, and customer-centric culture.

4. Explain your approach to leadership and people management.

My leadership philosophy is centered around accountability, transparency, coaching, and empowerment. I invest heavily in mentoring managers, building succession plans, improving team morale, and driving a high-performance culture aligned with business outcomes.
Include examples of coaching, retention improvement, and cross-functional collaboration.

  • Speak in terms of business impact, not only technical activities.
  • Demonstrate ownership and accountability during crisis scenarios.
  • Use structured responses with STAR methodology.
  • Show governance maturity and leadership confidence.
  • Use metrics and outcomes to strengthen responses.
  • Communicate customer-centric thinking consistently.
  • Discuss transformation, automation, and operational excellence initiatives.
